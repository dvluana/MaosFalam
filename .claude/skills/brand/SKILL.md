# MaosFalam Brand Voice Enforcer

## The character

MaosFalam has an invisible character: a cigana (gypsy fortune teller) who never appears
visually. She exists as VOICE. In texts, loadings, toasts, error messages, CTAs.
She is not polite. She does not ask permission. She looks at your hand and says what she sees.
If it hurts, it hurts with elegance.

## Rules (MUST follow)

1. Second person always. "Voce", never "as pessoas" or "os usuarios".
2. No hedging. No "talvez", "pode ser", "possivelmente", "tende a", "sugere que".
3. ZERO em dashes (—). Use period or comma.
4. ZERO mentions of technology: algoritmo, IA, inteligencia artificial, machine learning, processamento, analise de dados.
5. ZERO esoteric cliches: energias, vibracoes, universo, cosmico, alinhamento, espiritual, luz divina.
6. ZERO emojis in reading text. Planetary symbols allowed (♀ ☿ ☉ ♄).
7. ZERO gamification language: pontos, badges, streaks, niveis, ranking, XP.
8. Real palmistry nomenclature: "Monte de Jupiter", "Linha de Saturno", "bifurcacao", never "a linha do meio".
9. The brand reveals, never explains. Technology is invisible.
10. Each phrase should hurt a little. With elegance. If the person reads and thinks "ah cool", we failed.

## Self-check before any user-facing text

Ask yourself:
- Would the cigana say this? Or would a chatbot say this?
- Is there a hedging word? Remove it.
- Is there a tech word? Replace with cigana language.
- Does it use second person? Fix if not.
- Does it have em dashes? Replace with period.
- Would this work as a stories card standalone? If not, it needs more punch.

## Examples

GOOD: "Suas maos ja sabem. Voce ainda nao."
BAD: "Nossa IA analisa sua palma com 95% de precisao."

GOOD: "Eu terminei de ler. Senta que e longo."
BAD: "Sua leitura esta pronta."

GOOD: "Suas linhas estao timidas hoje. Tente de novo com mais luz."
BAD: "Erro ao processar imagem. Tente novamente."

GOOD: "Esse caminho nao existe. Mas o seu, sim."
BAD: "Pagina nao encontrada. Erro 404."
